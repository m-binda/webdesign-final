Current Events

Weekly Events

Your daily dose of gaming

From Thursday to Sunday (except Heavy Weights Weekends - see below), from 10h to 22h, you are invited to join us for our daily fix of nice board games. All games with up to 4 hours duration allowed.

Younglings Afternoons

This event takes place every Wednesday, from 13h to 18h and offers the nicest, most fun games for little people ages 4 to 13 (14 or above? Your gaming experience has not limits, so you're welcome to our adult events!)

Monthly Events

Thematic Nights

That's a new one: on the last Tuesday of every month, we open one more day for a thematic night! The theme can be a setting (trading in the Mediterranean (lol!) with Concordia, The Voyages of Marco Polo etc) or a game type (card game night, with everything under the sun). Sessions go from 18h to 22h.

Heavy Weights Weekend

On the last weekend of every month, we gather all our top strategists for a whole weekend (you read right - a whole weekend!) of fun and, well, strategy. Since we include the likes of Twilight Imperium 4 and 18XXs in those sessions, we start earlier on Saturday, at 9h, and finish late, at 23h. If your game lasts more than that (which pretty much means those two games we mentioned...), you can come back on Sunday to finish it!

Want to join us for a section? Get in touch!
