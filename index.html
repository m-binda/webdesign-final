Welcome to the Anima Mundi Board Game Club

What is this club about?

The club is located in Vit√≥ria, ES, Brazil, and it will be your next stop for a fun hobby to play with family and friends (old or new!). We are a club dedicated to modern board games (so forget about old, boring Monopoly and Risk), which will take you to the next level of fun.

This club is a not-for-profit organization headed by some very nice people who are just passionate about this hobby. Our goal is to be a safe place for hobbyists new and old (age and experience-wise). So not worries you have never played a modern board game: we've got you covered!

In our quite huge collection, we've got games for all tastes and ages. You have kids and want them to have a very fun activity for the afternoons? We hold special events for our little ones in the afternoons. You are an old-timer, thirsty for heavy games (yeah, even those 18XX games)? Do not worry: we have our monthly event for those heavy boxes to get dusted. Oh, yeah, and we do have snacks in the house.

So, again, welcome to our amazing Anima Mundi club! We are looking forward to seeing you here.
